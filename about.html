Who We Are?

Is there anything better than a beauty brand that is not just in touch with global issues but also makes an effort to create impact? The Touch is among the best makeup brands in India and is known for its high-quality makeup, yes, but also for its several attempts at driving change and advocating equality for women. It is an empowering company that makes one feel lucky to be associated with, as it brings a new dawn to the beauty sector.The brand believes in beauty as a motivating tool, and not just one that makes us aware of our perfect imperfections. It also believes in bringing out the best version of its consumers, further in line with its ethos of change, innovation, and empowerment.
